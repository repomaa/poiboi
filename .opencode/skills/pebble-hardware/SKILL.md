# pebble-hardware

> Physical hardware specifications for the new Pebble smartwatches: Pebble 2 Duo, Pebble Time 2, and Pebble Round 2.

## Overview

Pebble was revived in 2025 by Core Devices (founded by original creator Eric Migicovsky) after Google open-sourced most of PebbleOS in January 2025. Core Devices recovered the Pebble trademark in July 2025. A third model, the Pebble Round 2, was announced in January 2026.

All new Pebble devices share these characteristics:
- Always-on e-paper display (monochrome or color)
- IPX8 water resistance
- Microphone and speaker
- Vibration motor with haptic patterns
- Backlight
- Step tracking and sleep tracking
- Bluetooth connectivity to Android/iOS companion app
- Open-source PebbleOS
- Battery life of at least two weeks (up to 30 days on some models)

## Pebble 2 Duo

**Platform codename:** `emery`  
**Price:** $149  
**Shipping:** ~July 2025

### Display
- 1.26-inch monochrome e-paper screen
- Resolution: 144 × 168 pixels
- Always-on, transflective (readable in sunlight)

### Body
- Polycarbonate frame
- 22 mm strap width
- Four physical buttons (back, up, down, select)
- No touchscreen

### Sensors
- 6-axis inertial measurement unit (IMU)
- Compass (magnetometer)
- Barometer
- No heart rate monitor

### Battery & I/O
- Up to 30 days battery life
- USB-C magnetic charging dongle
- Quieter and stronger vibration motor than original Pebble 2

## Pebble Time 2

**Platform codename:** `gabbro`  
**Price:** $225  
**Shipping:** ~December 2025

### Display
- 1.5-inch 64-color e-paper screen
- Resolution: 200 × 228 pixels
- Larger screen than Duo; legacy watch faces may show a border until updated
- Always-on, transflective

### Body
- Aluminum frame
- 22 mm strap width
- Four physical buttons (back, up, down, select)
- **Capacitive touchscreen** (for tapping complications and supplemental input)

### Sensors
- 6-axis IMU
- Heart rate sensor
- No barometer

### Battery & I/O
- Up to 30 days battery life
- USB-C magnetic charging dongle
- Touchscreen allows tapping into apps from watch face complications

## Pebble Round 2

**Platform codename:** Likely `diorite`-class or similar round identifier  
**Price:** $199  
**Shipping:** ~May 2026

### Display
- 1.3-inch **round** color e-paper display
- Resolution: 260 × 260 pixels at 283 DPI
- Thickness: 8.1 mm (very slim)
- Much thinner bezel than the original Pebble Time Round
- Backlight for night visibility

### Body
- Stainless steel frame
- Three color options:
  - Matte black (20 mm quick-release band)
  - Silver (14 mm or 20 mm band)
  - Polished rose gold (14 mm band only)
- Four physical buttons
- **Capacitive touchscreen**

### Sensors
- Accelerometer
- Magnetometer (compass)
- No heart rate monitor

### Battery & I/O
- 10–14 days battery life
- Dual microphones for speech input and dictation
- Speaker for audio feedback
- USB-C magnetic charging dongle
